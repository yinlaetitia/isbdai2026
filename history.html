Conference Timeline

2024 4th International Symposium on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence (ISBDAI 2024) was held in HongKong (online and onsite) on Dec. 14-16 2024. Participants (online and onsite) are from USA, Austria, Italy, Germany, Switzerland, Netherlands, Kazakhstan, Cyprus, Romania, Australia, Thailand, Pakistan, Malaysia, Uzbekistan, Mainland China, Hong Kong, Macao and etc. Conference Proceedings was published by ACM International Conference Proceedings Series, which was indexed by Ei Compendex, Scopus, and ACM Digital Library.

2022 3rd International Symposium on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence (ISBDAI 2022) was held in Singapore (online and onsite) on Dec. 9-10 2022. Participants (online and onsite) are from Singapore, Cyprus, New Zealand, UK, USA, Italy, Romania, Australia, Thailand, Pakistan, Portugal, Mainland China, Hong Kong and etc. Conference Proceedings was published by ACM International Conference Proceedings Series, which was indexed by Ei Compendex, Scopus, and ACM Digital Library.

2020 2nd International Symposium on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence (ISBDAI 2020) was held in Johannesburg on October 15 - 16 2020. Participants (online) are from Democratic Republic Congo, UK, USA, Italy, Romania, Australia, Cyprus, South Africa, Mainland China, Hong Kong and etc. Conference Proceedings was published by ACM International Conference Proceedings Series, which was indexed by Ei Compendex, Scopus, and ACM Digital Library.

2018 International Symposium on Big Data and Artificial Intelligence (ISBDAI 2018) was held in Hong Kong on Dec 29 - 30 2018 (and also we have a parallel session in Guilin). Participants are from South Africa (University of Johannesburg, University of South Africa), Taiwan (Center for High Performance Computing), Hong Kong (Hong Kong Shue Yan University), Changsha (Changsha University of Technology), Beijing (Tsinghua University), Chengdu (Tencent), Guilin (Guilin University of Technology), Jinan (Shandong University of Finance and Economics), Japan (Ehime university, Meiji University), Kazakhstan (Eurasian National University), Malaysia (INWASCON), South Korea (Kunsan National University, Busan University of Foreign Studies), Sweden (56 Digital), USA (IDSAI) and etc. Conference Proceedings was published by ACM International Conference Proceedings Series, which was indexed by Ei Compendex, Scopus, ACM Digital Library, and CPCI / Web of Science.
