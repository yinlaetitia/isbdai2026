Call for Papers

Submit your research to ISBDAI 2026

The symposium is organized by the International Engineering and Technology Institute (IETI) and co-organized by the Institute for Higher Education Research and Development (IHERD) at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU).

It focuses on frontier topics in both the theoretical and applied aspects of Data Science, Cloud Computing, Mobile Web, IoT, Computer Science (CS), and Artificial Intelligence (AI), while particularly encouraging and supporting research that explores the intersection of these fields with education. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • AI-powered teaching tools
  • Data-driven educational decision-making
  • Cloud-based collaborative learning platforms
  • IoT-enabled smart campus systems
  • Ethical implications of technology in education
  • Integration of CS/AI fundamentals into educational curricula to foster digital literacy
More Information on Topics

As a leading international event, ISBDAI aims to provide an innovative platform for academic exchange among students, faculty members, researchers, and industry professionals from across the globe.

Publication

All presented papers will be published in the conference proceedings, while selected outstanding papers will be recommended to relevant journals for potential publication.

Submission Guidelines

Prospective authors are invited to submit full-length papers (up to 12 pages, single-column) adhering to the conference format via the Paper Submission System.

For more details on thematic areas, paper formatting, and submission guidelines, please visit the conference website or contact the organizing committee.
