About the Venue

The Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU) is a public research university located in Hung Hom, Hong Kong. Founded in 1937, PolyU is one of the most prestigious universities in Hong Kong and is recognized internationally for its excellence in research and teaching. The university's modern campus provides an ideal setting for academic conferences, offering state-of-the-art facilities and easy accessibility from Hong Kong's major transportation hubs.
